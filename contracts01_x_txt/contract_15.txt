The Provider shall continue to provide the Services during any notice period, unless otherwise agreed by the parties in writing. The provisions of this Agreement that by their nature are intended to survive termination shall remain in full force and effect following the termination of this Agreement, including but not limited to the provisions relating to confidentiality, indemnities, and liability limitations.
